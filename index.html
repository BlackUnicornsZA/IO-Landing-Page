What's VNM?

VNM is our platform token, a cryptocurrency that lives in every user's assigned digital wallet and can be traded for access to premium content, creative services, mobile data, merch, and gear. After our Beta, VMN will be traded for real-world and off-platform value. We realised that in order to create equity and creative freedom, we had to rethink the mechanics of the creative economy.
